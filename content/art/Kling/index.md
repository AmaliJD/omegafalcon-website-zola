I made this while listening to **Kontinuum**, the ending song for the anime _To Be Hero X!_

I'm not very happy with it though. The colors and lines are very messy, and blurry with glow. I was also imagining glowing hot lava flowing behind but didn't know how to exactly draw that. Also, the title _Kling_ looks and sounds stupid. Very dissapointed.

As I've grown as an artist, I've been finding it increasingly difficult to capture what I truly envision within Blender's Grase Pencil. Perhaps it's finally time to change drawing programs. I said that same thing about being a game dev with Unity lol.
