I challenged myself to make OmegaFalcon more expressive.

OmegaFalcon's "body" is pretty difficult for me to pose because it's sculpted from a very rgid cube shape. To add expressiveness, I contorted his body and "legs" so it actually looks like he's taking a stance. I drew eyebrows for more facial expression, a mouth to complete the more human appearance, and hands to really give proper form to his stance. Now he looks like he has a full body!

For the mouth, I wanted a design that wouldn't significantly change how the face looked. Something like the main Geometry Dash icon's rectangular mouth or a traditional cartoon face's mouth would have done that, it would make Omega look like a completely different character. What I drew is pretty fitting for Omega's skull design. I really like how it looks like a furnace cover, with Omega's inner fire raging within his body.

I also aimed to preserve that raw energy that gets lost when transiotioning from rough sketch to clean lineart and color.
