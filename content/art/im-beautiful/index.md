I drew **I’m Beautiful** believing it would be my final public drawing before I either killed myself or was forced offline. My previous drawings were reflections of my self-loathing; depictions of my evil, corrupted, disgusting and damned soul. These feelings always exist in maddening conflict against feelings of love, respect, and admiration for who I am.


In this moment, I decided to stop fighting love and let myself feel its full warming embrace. When I look at myself through a less societally-informed lens, removed from the problematic actions I've committed in my time on this Earth, I'm in love with what I see. I see my passion and my creativity, my morality, my love, my mind. I see a beautiful person.
